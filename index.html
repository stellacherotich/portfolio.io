About Me

I'm a junior data scientist/analyst with a passion for turning raw data into valuable insights. My expertise lies in Python, SQL, and data visualization, and I enjoy solving complex data challenges.
As a data whisperer, I have a knack for unraveling the hidden stories behind the numbers and presenting them in a way that inspires action. I'm eager to collaborate on exciting projects and drive data-informed decisions.
When I'm not getting secrets from data, you can find me exploring new places and getting immersed in a new book.

Projects

Twitter Sentiment Analysis
Using Natural Language Processing

This project leverages the power of Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze sentiments expressed on Twitter about two tech giants, Apple & Google. By understanding these sentiments, the project uncovers valuable insights, enabling both companies to make informed decisions to enhance their brand reputation and overall customer satisfaction.

Customer Churn Prediction
For a Telecommunications Company

This is a project focused on predicting customer churn using data analytics and machine learning. It aims to identify patterns in customer data to proactively retain customers and improve satisfaction. By understanding factors contributing to churn, businesses can implement targeted retention strategies.

Airline Customer Review
Sentiment Analysis

The project involves sentiment analysis of customer reviews for three different airlines. By categorizing sentiments as positive, negative, or neutral, the project seeks to gain valuable insights into customer opinions and experiences with each airline. The analysis could aid airlines in identifying strengths and weaknesses, addressing customer complaints, and ultimately enhancing their services to improve customer satisfaction.

Small Business

This project focuses on evaluating the creditworthiness of small businesses by utilizing data analysis and machine learning techniques to assess various financial and business-related factors, providing lenders and financial institutions with valuable insights for making informed decisions on loan applications from small businesses.

Diabetes Prevention
Hackathon Solution

This project uses data science and machine learning techniques in order to predict diabetes risks for people based on their health information. It also provides helpful advice for managing diabetes through lifestyle changes and steps that can be taken to advance the solutions that are being developed.
